# Presentation Script: EnviroSense
**Time Limit:** 3-4 Minutes (Plus Demo)
**Theme:** AI for Social Good

---

## 1. The Hook: The Invisible Crisis (30 Seconds)
**"What if your anxiety isn't just in your head? What if it's in the air?"**

*   **The Problem:** Mental health disorders are rising globally. In Bucharest, this correlates with some of the worst air quality in Europe.
*   **The Gap:** Medicine treats the *symptom* (anxiety). Environmental science tracks the *cause* (pollution). **Nothing connects the two.**
*   **The Consequence:** Patients are medicated without addressing the environmental root cause.

---

## 2. The Solution: Eco-Neuro Sentinel (30 Seconds)
**"A Neuro-Symbolic Health Monitor that listens to you and watches the sky."**

We built a platform that correlates **Subjective Biological Data** (how you feel) with **Objective Environmental Telemetry** (PM2.5, NO2) in real-time.

*   **For the Patient:** It's a "Digital Twin" that warns them when environmental factors might trigger a health episode.
*   **For the Doctor:** It provides context. Instead of just seeing "Patient is anxious," they see "Patient is anxious AND PM2.5 levels in Sector 1 are 4x the safe limit."

---

## 3. The Core Workflow: Human-Centric AI (45 Seconds)
**"AI doesn't replace the doctor. It prepares the room."**

*   **The Agent:** Listens to the patient and checks EU Safety Directives (RAG).
*   **The Action:** If the risk is high, it doesn't just chat—it **acts**. It compiles a clinical summary and alerts a human specialist.
*   **The Soft Handoff:** The doctor steps in with a complete picture, ready to treat the patient, not just the data.

*(Transition: "Let's see this 'Soft Handoff' in action...")*

---

## 4. Under the Hood: Technical Innovation (30 Seconds)
*   **Agentic Workflow:** The AI autonomously queries the database to find the right specialist based on symptom keywords.
*   **RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation):** Every piece of advice is grounded in the **EU Directive 2024/2881**. The AI cannot hallucinate safety standards.
*   **Privacy by Design:** We use an "Airlock" pattern. No raw PII is ever sent to the AI model.

---

## 5. Business & Viability (45 Seconds)
**"High Impact, Sustainable Revenue."**

**Operational Efficiency:**
*   **Hybrid-AI:** A lightweight ML model filters low-risk cases, reducing costs to **$0.008 per interaction**.

**Revenue Model (B2B2C):**
1.  **Insurance (Primary):** Licensing fee for high-risk patients to reduce ER visits (ROI: 5x).
2.  **Municipalities (Secondary):** Selling aggregated "Health-Impact Maps" for urban planning.
3.  **Freemium (Tertiary):** Consumer subscription for "clean air routing."

**"Why Eco-Neuro Sentinel is a Category Definer."**

We are not just another health app; we are the **missing link** between Environmental Science and Clinical Medicine.

1.  **From Reactive to Predictive:**
